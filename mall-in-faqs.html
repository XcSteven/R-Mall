Frequently Asked Questions

1. How can I find a product?

Answer: In order to see a desired product, click on the Browse tab on the top navigation bar. Once you are on the Browse page, choose a store you want to view the product from, and proceed from there.

2. Can I add a product to a Wishlist?

Answer: Unfortunately, we are still in the early stage of developing the website, so Wishlist is not available yet for commercial use.

3. Apart from the Contact form available on the website, who can I contact for urgent purposes?

Answer: We are more than welcome to hear from you. You can contact us through e-mail for a quick reponse. Send Email

4. What if the groceries purchased from the Food Store has gone bad, how can you solve this problem?

Answer: We only ship food that would not gone bad in a short period of time. However, if you find a bad product in your order, please contact us via Contact tab on our website for a refund.

5. What is your mall's opening hour?

Answer: As an online shopping center, we do not have a distinct opening/closing hour. However, our active time (responding customers, shipping orders, etc.) spans from 8 AM to 10 PM every day. We would still be active earlier or later than that, but if you want a quicker reponse from us, please kindly contact us during those hours, thank you.
